# EFIR Teacher Database - Extracted from efiriyad.com (March 2026)

## Key Finding: Titulaires vs Local Staff

The school structure page states the school has **"personnels titulaires de l'Education Nationale, dont 24 enseignants"** (French government-appointed staff, including 24 teachers). These titulaires are **paid by France's Ministry of Education**, NOT by the school. They are therefore **NOT in the staff master data Excel** (which only tracks locally-hired payroll).

**~27 teachers on the website are NOT in the master data** - these are almost certainly the titulaires.
