# EFIR / AEFE Curriculum: Subjects (Matieres) by Level

> Compiled: March 2026
> Sources: education.gouv.fr, eduscol.education.fr, efiriyad.com, legifrance.gouv.fr
> Context: EFIR follows the French national curriculum (Education Nationale) as an AEFE-homologated
> school. The host-country language (Arabic) and English are integrated per AEFE guidelines.

## CYCLE 1: MATERNELLE (PS, MS, GS)

Total weekly instruction: **24 hours** (including ~30 min daily recess)

Maternelle does not use traditional "subjects" (matieres). Teaching is organized around
**5 domaines d'apprentissage** (learning domains). No fixed hourly grid per domain exists
at the national level -- teachers organize flexibly. The domains are:

### 5 Domaines d'apprentissage

| #   | Domaine (official name)                                          | Abbrev.    | Notes                                             |
| --- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Mobiliser le langage dans toutes ses dimensions                  | Langage    | Oral + written. Priority domain across all levels |
| 2   | Agir, s'exprimer, comprendre a travers l'activite physique       | EPS        | Min 30-45 min daily                               |
| 3   | Agir, s'exprimer, comprendre a travers les activites artistiques | Arts       | Visual arts, music, drama                         |
| 4   | Acquerir les premiers outils mathematiques                       | Maths      | Numbers, shapes, logic                            |
| 5   | Explorer le monde                                                | Decouverte | Time, space, living things, matter, digital tools |

### EFIR-specific additions (language du pays hote)

| Level | Arabic                                       | English                             |
| ----- | -------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| PS    | 5x30min bilingual immersion + 3x40min Arabic | None                                |
| MS    | 5x30min bilingual immersion + 3x40min Arabic | None                                |
| GS    | 4x25min bilingual + 4x40min Arabic           | 1x25min bilingual + 1x40min English |

## NATIONAL EXAMS

### Diplome National du Brevet (DNB) -- end of 3eme

Tested subjects:

- Francais (100 points)
- Mathematiques (100 points)
- Histoire-geographie-EMC (50 points)
- Sciences (2 subjects drawn from: SVT, Physique-chimie, Technologie) (50 points)
- Oral de projet (100 points)
- Controle continu (400 points across all subjects)

### Baccalaureat General -- end of Terminale

Evaluated:

- Epreuve anticipee de Francais (1ere): ecrit + oral
- Philosophie (Terminale): ecrit
- Grand oral (Terminale)
- 2 epreuves de specialite (Terminale)
- Controle continu: Histoire-geo, LVA, LVB, Enseignement scientifique, EPS, EMC

EFIR 2024 results: 99% success rate (74/75 candidates)
